## Why "wait" is a coin flip (honest recalibration)

- Gain from waiting (loan paydown > depreciation): **+$4,680** over ~10 mo.
- Cost of waiting (full Kia operating premium ~$455/mo × 10): **−$4,550**.
- **Net ≈ +$130.** And the ~$9k negative equity is *already sunk* — waiting doesn't
  erase it; switching doesn't create it. So dollars don't decide; **risk/hassle do**.

## Sensitivity

- **Office charging capped/lost** → every EV degrades hard ($58→$184/mo); keep-Kia
  or a hybrid/PHEV wins. *Validate the charger first.*
- **High insurance** ($360–425 Tesla/BMW) → widens the Ioniq 5's lead; BMW never recovers to #1.
- **Gas normalizes to $4.50** → Kia's only weakness shrinks; lengthen the wait.
- **Holding period short → LEASE** (Ioniq 5, clean exit); **long → BUY** (Ioniq 5 at
  0%, or new Model Y for the deduction). The buy-vs-lease flip hinges almost entirely
  on this unknown.
